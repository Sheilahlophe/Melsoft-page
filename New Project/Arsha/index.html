Where Tech Careers Take Flight

Melsoft Academy is an extension of Melsoft, a multi-award-winning software engineering company with a proven track record for excellence.Fueled by the same innovative spirit and expertise that made Melsoft a standout in the tech industry, our academy is on a mission to address youth unemployment in Africa by equipping students with essential tech skills, work experience, and job placements. Our specialized bootcamp-style programs are designed to empower the next generation of software developers, focusing on practical, hands-on experience, and community engagement. Through income-share agreements and job guarantees, we are committed to removing financial barriers and opening doors to fulfilling and lucrative tech careers for youth across the continent.
